7 fun facts about baby Baby Pigs!

  1. New born piglets weigh about 2.5 pounds
  2. Pigs are usually black, yellow, or brown
  3. Piglets learn to run towards their mother's voices
  4. Newborn baby piglets will leave their nest to use the toilet only hours after they have been born
  5. Piglets can be trained to learn their own names at just two weeks old. They are so smart!
  6. Piglets are very sensitive to the cold
  7. There are around 675 million pigs in the world

7 foods to Feed Piglets!

  1. Sweets and high-sugar foods
  2. Dog food
  3. Cracked corn
  4. Milk
  5. Fish
  6. Meat
  7. Fruits
